# Tournament Structure for Werewolves Game

## Tournament Flow and Scoring

1. **Registration**  
   Players must register before the tournament begins. A maximum of 16 players can participate.

2. **Preliminary Rounds**  
   - Each match consists of two games played simultaneously.  
   - The player who wins both games advances to the next round.
   - If each player wins one game, a tiebreaker game is played.

3. **Elimination Rounds**  
   - The tournament will follow a single-elimination format from the quarterfinals onward.  
   - Winners move to the semifinals and then to the finals.

4. **Scoring**  
   - Players earn points based on their performance in matches:
     - Win: 3 points
     - Loss: 0 points
     - Tiebreaker win: 1 additional point

5. **Final Match**  
   - The final match is a best-of-three series.  
   - The tournament champion is crowned based on the total points accumulated.
